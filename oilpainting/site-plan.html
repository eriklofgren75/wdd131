Oil Paintings Site Plan

Erik Lofgren

Purpose

This website showcases my original oil paintings to share my paintings with friends/family and other enthusiasts. It may also work toward business relationships where people could see my work and get my contact information.

Audience

Art lovers, friends and family, local galleries, and anyone interested in discovering or purchasing original oil paintings.

Dynamic Elements Description

  • Interactive gallery: filter paintings by category (e.g., contains people, snow, water, etc.) using JavaScript arrays and the filter method.
  • Lightbox feature: click a painting to open a larger view with painting details using DOM manipulation and event listeners.
  • Code organization: ES modules for gallery and form scripts, usage of objects and arrays to store painting data, and array methods like forEach to render gallery items.

Logo

I have a logo that looks good for these purposes. I used image generators for it.

Colors

  • Background: #FAF3E0 (soft cream)
  • Primary: #8A3B12 (deep burnt sienna)
  • Secondary: #556B2F (muted olive green)
  • Text: #333333 (dark charcoal)

Fonts

  • Header: Playfair Display (serif)
  • Body: Open Sans (sans-serif)

Content

Home Page

  • Welcome message introducing myself as an oil painter.
  • Slideshow of 3-5 featured paintings.
  • Short artist statement describing my style and inspiration.

Gallery Page

  • Filter paintings by category (contains water, etc).
  • Responsive grid of painting thumbnails with titles.
  • Click to open painting in lightbox/modal with details.
